About Me

Hey there! I am Liqian, a AI/ML researcher with design background. Currently mastering at NYU's Tandon School of Engineering, I dive deep into deep learning, coding, and virtual reality.

My journey includes being a Course Assistant, where I get to share my passion and knowledge with classmates, and a stint as a Graduate Research Assistant, not only contributed to the academic community but also indulged my problem-solving desire by navigating the challenges of audio generation and separation. I've also coordinated production for top brands in a past life, bringing ideas to life from concept to creation.

With projects like "Efficient Video BGM Generation" and "VAHA" under my belt, I'm all about leveraging AI to make the world a bit more creative and connected. My toolkit? A mix of Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and a sprinkle of 3D modeling and AR/VR development, etc..

I speak fluent English, Mandarin, and Cantonese. Driven by a passion for discovery and innovation, my goal is to contribute to research that addresses complex challenges and advances the field of Deep Learning. Let's chat and explore the unknown together!
