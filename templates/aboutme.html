About Me

I am an innovative and versatile professional with a strong background in fashion design and a passion for exploring the cutting-edge fields of AI/ML and computer vision. As a proud graduate of the Fashion Institute of Technology, I hold a Bachelor's degree in Fashion Design, and I am currently pursuing my Master's in Integrated Design and Media at NYU Tandon School of Engineering.

In my previous role as a Production Coordinator at One to 13 Studio, I honed my skills in sample-making and production management for prestigious brands such as Thom Browne, Vera Wang, Jason Wu, and Adam Lippes. My ability to thrive under pressure and effectively manage teams during high-stakes runway seasons ensured timely delivery of exquisite finished samples. My keen eye for detail and problem-solving capabilities were instrumental in addressing and resolving issues, such as pattern errors and trim shortages, maintaining smooth production processes.

Now, as I venture into AI/ML and computer vision, I am excited to merge my design background with these emerging technologies to create innovative solutions and contribute to the ever-evolving world of design. My unique blend of experience in fashion and passion for technology positions me as a forward-thinking professional, eager to make a lasting impact in the industry.
